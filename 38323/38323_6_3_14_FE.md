### 6.3.14 FE

Length: 1 bit

Indication of whether checksum error is detected or not. Value \'1\'
means checksum error is detected and the UE shall reset the compression
buffer.

Table 6.3.14-1: FE field

  --------- -------------------------------------------------------------
   **Bit**                         **Description**

      0                               No Error

      1                      Checksum Error Notification
  --------- -------------------------------------------------------------
